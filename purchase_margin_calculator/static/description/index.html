Purchase Margin Calculator

Track the real purchase cost, convert between UoMs, and monitor margin health on every product.

Odoo Version16.0
LicenseOPL-1
AuthorNeuroDev
CategoryInventory / Purchase
Dependenciesproduct, purchase

Key Features

Last Purchase Cost Tracking
Automatically captures the last confirmed purchase price, vendor, date, and UoM for each product.
UoM Conversion
If the purchase UoM differs from the sale UoM (e.g., buy in kg, sell in units), the cost is automatically converted so the margin comparison is apples-to-apples.
Margin Analysis & Alerts
Computes the effective sale price (POS pricelist or list price), calculates margin %, and flags products with color-coded alerts:

OK Healthy margin (above target factor)
Warning Margin below target but above 10%
Critical Margin below 10% of cost
No Data No purchase history yet
Purchase Price History
Keeps the last 10 purchase entries per product with date, vendor, order reference, original price, purchase UoM, and converted price. Populated automatically on install from existing confirmed POs.
Configurable Margin Factor
Set the target margin factor in Purchase Settings (default 1.30 = 30% markup). The suggested price and alert thresholds adjust automatically.
Daily Cron Recalculation
A scheduled action recalculates costs and alerts daily. You can also trigger a manual recalculation from any product form.
Safe by design: This module does NOT overwrite standard_price. Your existing costing method (FIFO, Average, Standard) remains untouched.

Where to find it

  • Product form → new tab Purchase Cost & Margin
  • Product list → optional columns: Last Cost, Suggested, Margin %, Alert
  • Search filters → Critical Margin, Warning Margin, Healthy Margin, No Purchase Data
  • Settings → Purchase → Purchase Margin Calculator section
